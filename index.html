Who is Tyler

Tyler is a name known far and wide for being the best friend out there, but who is he actually?

  • A compassionate friend ready with a listening ear.
  • A perfect hybrid between comic relief and some heartfelt advice.
  • A sure companion for any outdoor adventure.

Tyler was raised in Arizona and currently lives in Utah. No matter the time of year, you can always find him on the lake or with his friends. He loves his family more than anything else in this world and wants nothing if not to make his parents proud. He is pursuing a degree in strategic management in the Marriott School of Business and striving to love life while he is at it.

Family is Everything

Tyler is the oldest of three children and the the grateful reciepent of the title "Favorite Child"

Tyler is more passionate about his family than anything else you can find on this list of interests. He wants to follow in his father's footsteps when it comes to career paths and calls his mother more than any son probably should. He looks up to and is so grateful for his younger siblings. He especially loves to get his family into big projects or ambitious party planning.

Best Friends Forever

Probably one of the few people to have a best friend with his same name.

One of Tyler's favorite things to say is that he would rather go shopping with a best friend than go to the moon with a person he just tolerates. Of all of the things Tyler could be doing, it doesn't really matter to him, the only thing of importance is WHO is with him.

Conversion to Jesus Christ

Served as a missionary for Jesus Christ over two years on two continents

A member of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints, Tyler is a disciple of the Savior before anything else. Still striving to be a more devout follower each day, he knows that he is not perfect but continues to do his best to emulate the Savior's example.

Get outside!

Being from Arizona, sunsets and lake days are the way to Tyler's heart

A breath of fresh air is almost as vital to Tyler's well-being as a drink of cool water. Getting outside and especially into the unknown is a way to satisfy Tyler's bug for variety and change in his life.

Always have at least one flight booked

Tyler gets his love for traveling from his dad, who flew over 160 flights in a calendar year.

Tyler's philosophy is to always have at least one flight booked at all times. It keeps anticipation for life high and also helps to keep one's perspective in check when life gets difficult.

Mainland China

6 weeks working in Mainland China as an english teacher, not only including an all expenses paid week of travel, but also 4 weeks of paid work with young children.

  • The feeling that only comes with impacting a child's life in significant ways.
  • Experience the lush jungles of the 'lake province' Hangzhou.

Costa Rica

Whether you are looking for exotic animals, a relaxing beach, or to connect with nature, Costa Rica has it all for you.

  • Spend time at the bases of Costa Rica's many volcanoes to relax in the natural hot springs
  • Visit a nature reserve to play with monkeys, get personal with sloths, or watch the macaw's fly.
  • Stay on the beach to experience the lavish resorts and the many offered ammenities.

London, UK

Appreciate history as you walk the streets of a city that has influenced nearly every corner of the world in the last few centuries.

  • Visit Buckingham Palace and see the royal family and experience the heritage of this great city.
  • Venture into the countryside and visit cities like Bath to get a taste of ancient Roman construction.
  • Stay in the city and feel the spirit in some of the places that missionaries first preached overseas.
